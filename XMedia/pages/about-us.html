Looking for photography jobs?

Are you a photographer with an experience in working with paying clients? Are you looking for ways to gain new photography jobs? Our members get: - access to photography assignments, photography competitions, and photography related information (no spam) - access to a contact form which allows you to submit your portfolio if you are interested in working with us - access to a closed Facebook group where we also post assignments - possibility to be listed as our photographer agent worldwide It's exclusively for members. Once you become a member you are automatically added to our mailing list.
